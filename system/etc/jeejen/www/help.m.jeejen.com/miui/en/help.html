Basic operation
  • Tap and swipe
    Tap an item to open it or trigger its function. To swipe, quickly move your finger across the surface of the screen. The swipe gesture is often used to navigate between screens.
  • Long press and drag
    Long press an item on the screen by touching it and not lifting your finger until it responds. To drag an item, touch & hold it for a moment and then, without lifting your finger, move your finger accross screen until you reach your target position.
Home screen
  • To view different apps on your Home screen, swipe left or right.
  • To open an app, tap the corresponding icon on your Home screen.
Phone
  • Call log
    Missed calls are highlighted in red.
    To view a contact's info, tap his or her name.
    To place a call, tap 'Dial'.
  • Select and save contacts
    At the dial pad screen, tap 'Save' to save a number you've already entered.
Messaging
  • Messages
    Unread messages are highlighted in red.
    To view messages from one of your contacts, tap his or her name.
    To write a new message, tap 'New message'.
  • Conversations
    To increase a message's font size, tap it.
    To have a message read aloud to you, tap the Speaker icon.
  • Writing a message
    Tap 'New Message', enter the recipient's phone number or select a number from your contacts, then write or record your message.
Contacts
  • Search for a contact
    You can search for a contact using the first letter of their name.
  • Adding a new contact
    Enter your contact's name, then enter their phone number below.
